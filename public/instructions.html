How to Use Pixel Kingdom

Welcome to Pixel Kingdom! Here is how to use the site:

  • Click on a cell or click & drag to select multiple cells.
  • Release the mouse to open the purchase popup.
  • Fill in a title (required) and optionally text, color, link, or image.
  • After purchase, your cells will display the chosen content.
  • Use the search bar to find your purchased cells by title or coordinates.
  • You can zoom in/out using your mouse wheel.
