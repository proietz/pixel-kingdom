How to Use Pixel Kingdom

- Click on a cell to select it, or click and drag to select multiple cells.
- Use the popup to fill in title (required), text, color, link, or image.
- Zoom in/out with the mouse wheel.
- You can search by keyword or by coordinates in the search bar.
- Cells purchased will show their title and coordinates after buying.
- Price: 1€ per cell.
- The grid is always black initially; only purchased cells show color.
